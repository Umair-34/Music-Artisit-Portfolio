PHOEBE GREEN

making music since 2014

Phoebe Green is a 22 years old singer artist and music composer. Wrapped with a guitar around herself, she is immersed into
notes of music when singing or even composing souilful songs. Phoebe writes and composes her songs related to intense
and pure love influenced by incidents or things happening around her. Her songs are very personal to what she always relate
to and it is a heartfelt feeling when the audience appreciates what she does. She has been composing songs since she was
a teenager. Her love for music is sempiternal which is depicted through her songs. She is new in the music industry and she
has a dream and vision to become one of the famous leading singers globally.

Phoebe has been doing self releases and there are few singles that has been up to mark. She is gaining popularity slowly but
surely. There were some concerts where she participated and has been singing her few singles; ‘Dreaming of’, ‘Reinvent’ and
‘Easy Peeler’ has been few of her songs that were very much appreciated lately. She wants to eventually spread her music
globally so that everyone enjoys her own version of pop music. Make sure to subscribe to her youtube channel and follow her
on Instagram and Facebook for more of her content. She hopes to see your support through this so that she can continue to
make her music. Share her content on social media to boost her popularity if you like what she does!
